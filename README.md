# Seizing Iran's Uranium: The Physics of an Impossible Operation

An interactive explainer examining the unprecedented military, chemical, and nuclear risks involved in a hypothetical US operation to seize Iran's highly enriched uranium stockpile.

## What this covers

- **Three nuclear sites**: Isfahan, Natanz, and Fordow — distances, current status, and access challenges
- **Four operational phases**: from locating the material to handling it underground
- **The trilemma**: three options for dealing with uranium hexafluoride (UF₆), each with detailed risk profiles
- **The chemistry**: why UF₆, hydrogen fluoride, and uranyl fluoride make every option dangerous
- **Criticality risk**: the hidden danger most people don't know about
- **Breaking context**: the March 2026 Marine Expeditionary Unit deployment to the Middle East
